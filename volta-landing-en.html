How it works

Your training,
analyzed every week.
Automatically.

Volta connects with your device, reads your activities, asks the right questions and gives you a real analysis — not just numbers, but what they mean for your progress. Because improving your times depends on training well, in the real life you have.

📊

Automatic weekly analysis

Every week Volta compares what you did with your last 4 weeks. It tells you if you improved, slowed down, or if your load is well calibrated — automatically.

💬

A check-in that listens

Every Sunday Volta asks how you felt. If you mention a discomfort, it asks more — since when, where, what happened. Detects injury signals before they become a problem.

📈

Complete monthly report

At the end of the month you get a report with your full evolution: distance, pace, heart rate, sport trends and monthly alerts.

🎯

Adapts to your real life

You traveled, had a tough week at work, felt tired. Volta knows it and considers it. The plan that works is the one that adapts to the life you actually have.

📱

All on Telegram

No new apps. No platforms to learn. Volta lives on Telegram — where you already are. The weekly check-in takes less than 2 minutes.
