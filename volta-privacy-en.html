Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Version 1.0

1. Who We Are

Volta is a sports performance analysis platform for runners, cyclists and triathletes, accessible at getvolta.pro. The data controller is Volta, a sports technology platform operated from Colombia.

For any questions related to this policy, please write to us at .

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide you with our training analysis service:

  • Identification data: full name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Telegram contact data: your Telegram user ID, required to send you the weekly check-in and performance reports.
  • Sports and health activity data: training information obtained through the Garmin Connect API, with your explicit prior authorization. This includes distance, time, pace, heart rate, effort and activity type.
  • Sports profile data: primary sport, training goal and available days per week, provided during onboarding.
  • Check-in conversational data: your responses to the weekly check-in about sensations, physical discomfort, training adherence and upcoming events.
Volta does not collect payment data or real-time geolocation data. The health data we process (heart rate, effort and activity metrics) comes exclusively from your Garmin device through its official API. This data is never sold, never used for advertising, and is not shared with third parties without your prior explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to provide you with the Volta service:

  • Analyze your weekly training and compare it with your last 4 weeks of history.
  • Send you the weekly check-in via Telegram every Sunday and process your responses.
  • Detect signs of overload or injury from your activity data and check-in responses.
  • Generate your monthly report with your performance evolution, trends and alerts by sport.

Volta does not use your data for advertising, nor does it sell or share it with third parties for commercial purposes. Your Garmin data is only visible to you.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your data is based on:

  • Your explicit consent when registering for Volta and authorizing access to your Garmin Connect account.
  • Performance of the service you request — without this data we cannot provide you with training analysis.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by writing to us at . Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

4b. Special Consent for Health Data

Heart rate, effort and physiological metrics obtained through Garmin Connect constitute health data and receive special treatment:

  • Separate explicit consent: by authorizing the connection with Garmin Connect, you grant specific consent for the processing of your health data, independent from the general service consent.
  • Limited use: this data is only used to analyze your sports performance and generate reports. It is never used for advertising, commercial profiling or training of external AI models.
  • Never sold: under no circumstances will your health data be sold, transferred or monetized.
  • Exclusive access: your health data is only accessible to you. No other user, coach or third party can see it without your explicit authorization.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

To provide the service, we use the following technology providers who may have access to your data as data processors:

  • Garmin Connect API — source of sports activity data, with your prior authorization.
  • Telegram Bot API — communication channel for the weekly check-in and reports.
  • Google Sheets — storage of your profile and training data.
  • Google Gemini API — natural language processing for the conversational check-in.
  • Digital Ocean — server infrastructure where Volta operates.
  • Zoho Mail — sending of service emails.

All these providers operate under their own privacy policies and are subject to confidentiality commitments. No data is sold or shared for advertising purposes.

If we change any technology provider in the future, we will update this policy and notify you before the change takes effect.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Profile data: while you have an active account on Volta.
  • Check-in data: up to 12 months from registration, to generate evolution analysis.
  • Garmin data: we do not store an activity history beyond the last 8 weeks needed for comparative analysis.

When you delete your account, all your data is deleted from our systems within a maximum of 30 days.

If you revoke access to Garmin Connect from your Garmin account without deleting your Volta account, we will delete all activity data obtained through that integration within a maximum of 48 hours. You can reconnect Garmin at any time.

7. Your Rights

In accordance with Colombian data protection law (Law 1581 of 2012) and applicable regulations in LATAM, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify incorrect or outdated data.
  • Delete your personal data from our systems.
  • Revoke consent for the processing of your data.
  • Portability — request a copy of your data in a readable format.
  • Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, write to us at . We will respond within a maximum of 15 business days.

8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss or alteration:

  • All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS, ensuring your data travels securely at all times.
  • Garmin access tokens are stored securely and renewed automatically.
  • Data access is restricted exclusively to personnel necessary to operate the service.

In the event of a security breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you within the legally established timeframe.

9. Minors

Volta is intended exclusively for persons over 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, contact us at and we will delete that data immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of the document and, if the changes are significant, we will notify you by email or Telegram.

We recommend reviewing this page periodically. Continued use of the service after posting changes implies your acceptance of the updated policy.
